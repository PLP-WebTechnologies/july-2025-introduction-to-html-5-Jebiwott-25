Popular Snacks

Smokie Pasua

A boiled sausage sliced open and filled with kachumbari - a favorite street snack across Kenya.

Bhajias

Deep-fried potato slices coated in gram flour batter, served with chutney or tomato sauce.

Mutura

Kenya's African sausage, made from minced meat, spices, and herbs stuffed in an intestine casing.

Smocha

A delicious street wrap made by rolling a smokie inside a chapati, often served with choma sauce, tomato sauce or kachumbari.

Boiled Eggs (Mayai)

A quick and filling snack, often served with kachumbari, sauce or salt

Street Food Culture in Kenya

Street food in Kenya is more than just a meal - it's a way of life. From bustling city streets to quiet town corners, street vendors bring affordable, tasty, and unique flavors to everyone.

Fun Fact

Mutura is often enjoyed during evenings or night and you can never have enough of smokies.
